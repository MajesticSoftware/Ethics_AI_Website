Ethical Issues

The use of WormGPT raises significant ethical concerns. It lowers the barrier to entry for cybercriminals and can lead to a substantial increase in cybercrime, affecting individuals, businesses, and governments.

Regulatory Challenges

Regulating the misuse of generative AI like WormGPT is complex. The anonymity and accessibility of the internet complicate enforcement efforts, and international cooperation is essential for effective regulation.
